BrainKrig3.0 FAQs

Question: Can anyone register for BrainKrig?
Answer: Yes, but anyone who intends to register for BrainKrig must do so through their school. We don't accept registrations from students or parents without involvement of their school.

Question: Is the registration fee per school?
Answer: No. Registration fee is N3,000 PER CANDIDATE.

Question: Is there a minimum or maximum number of candidates that must register from a school?
Answer: No. You can register as few or as many as you want to.

Question: When and where is the program happening?
Answer: 9:00am on Friday, 23rd of May, 2025, at His Grace Multipurpose Hall, Idimu Road, Ejigbo Lagos.

About BLI

Budding Leaders International is an educational initiative dedicated to bridging the gap in traditional education systems that primarily emphasize academics. We focus on empowering young minds with essential skills, knowledge, and resources that extend beyond the classroom. Our programs are designed to equip the next generation with practical skill acquisition opportunities, fostering confidence, leadership, and personal growth.

Our Vision

To be a leading force in transforming education by nurturing young minds through skill acquisition, empowerment, and holistic learning experiences that prepare them for lifelong success.

Our Mission

Our mission is to enlighten, inspire, and empower young people by providing them with the education and practical skills needed to excel in all aspects of life. Through innovative learning approaches, mentorship, and access to valuable resources, we aim to create well-rounded individuals who are prepared to thrive beyond academic settings.
